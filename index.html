About Me

I am just a guy focused on one thing. My biggest strength revolves around systems thinking, holistic integration and cross-domain strategy.

My real journey in life began during a certain worldwide epidemic that forced me to reflect and commit to a life defined by intellectual curiosity and purpose. Since then, I've developed my own learning philosophy, personal development schemas, theoretical frameworks, failed projects and promising programs that galvanise me daily with clear ways to flow and the sustained disposition to bring forth a good world.
